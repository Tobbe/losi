*-----------------------------------------------------------------------------*

    SLI Theme Manager v2.0 Introduction

*-----------------------------------------------------------------------------*

 Requirements:
 **************
 
 A (probably unofficial) OTS3 Installation or
 LOSI x.x or
 Omars Litestep Installer 3.0.2 or
 A other, fully OTS2 compatible, Litestep Installer
 
 
 Installation:
 **************
 
 Simply copy SLI-ThemeManager.exe in "$LitestepDir$Utilities\".
 Run it.
 
 
 ReadMe:
 ********
 
 Open the last Tab in SLI-ThemeManager for more details.
